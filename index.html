Who We Are

Meet The Maps Agency

The Maps Agency was crafted from years of newsrooms, sets, and stories — where we learned that the most powerful content is the kind that moves people.

Founded by Emmy Award–winning journalist María Paula Espinosa, Maps was born from a deep belief that every brand deserves content that doesn't just exist — it resonates, it performs, and it lasts.

We are a team of creatives who think like strategists and execute like producers. Maps exists for the brands that refuse to blend in.

We create content with intention, execute with precision, and deliver with excellence.
